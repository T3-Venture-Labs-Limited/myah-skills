# Productivity Starter Pack

The essential collection for anyone looking to streamline their workday and get more done with Myah.

## What's Included

### Myah Welcome
Your onboarding companion. Learn what Myah can do and get personalized setup guidance.

### Calendar Weekly Planner  
Transform chaotic schedules into organized weeks. Get smart suggestions for focus time, meeting prep, and conflict resolution.

### Inbox Zero
Tame your email with intelligent triage, draft replies, and systematic inbox management. Achieve and maintain inbox zero.

### Daily Standup
Generate professional standup reports automatically from your calendar and tasks. Perfect for agile teams and remote workers.

## Who This Is For

- New Myah users getting started
- Busy professionals managing multiple priorities
- Remote workers needing structure
- Anyone looking to reclaim their time

## Best Practices

1. Start with **Myah Welcome** to learn the basics
2. Use **Calendar Weekly Planner** every Sunday evening or Monday morning
3. Process email with **Inbox Zero** in dedicated blocks
4. Generate **Daily Standup** reports each morning
