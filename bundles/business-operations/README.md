# Business Operations Suite

Professional tools for managing the operational side of your business — from meetings to expenses to team coordination.

## What's Included

### Meeting Follow-Up
Never lose track of what was decided. Generate meeting summaries, extract action items, and draft follow-up emails automatically.

### Receipt Organizer
Extract data from receipts, categorize expenses, and generate professional expense reports. Stay on top of your finances effortlessly.

### Inbox Zero
Process emails efficiently with smart triage, draft replies, and systematic inbox management. Keep communications organized.

### Calendar Weekly Planner
Plan your week by reviewing upcoming events, suggesting time blocks, and helping prioritize tasks. Optimize your schedule.

## Who This Is For

- Business owners managing operations
- Operations managers coordinating teams
- Freelancers tracking expenses and client communications
- Anyone who wants professional-grade business tools

## Best Practices

1. Use **Meeting Follow-Up** immediately after every meeting
2. Process receipts with **Receipt Organizer** within 24 hours
3. Check **Inbox Zero** twice daily — morning and afternoon
4. Review your week every Sunday with **Calendar Weekly Planner**
