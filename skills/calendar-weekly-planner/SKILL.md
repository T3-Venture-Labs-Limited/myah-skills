# Calendar Weekly Planner

Plan your week effectively by reviewing upcoming events, blocking focus time, and prioritizing what matters most.

## Features

- **Week overview** — See all meetings, deadlines, and commitments at a glance
- **Focus time blocks** — Suggest uninterrupted work periods based on your schedule
- **Task prioritization** — Help rank tasks by urgency and importance
- **Conflict detection** — Identify overlapping commitments or unrealistic workloads
- **Prep reminders** — Alert you to prepare for upcoming meetings

## How It Works

1. **Review** — Scan your calendar for the upcoming week
2. **Analyze** — Identify busy periods, gaps, and potential conflicts
3. **Suggest** — Recommend time blocks for deep work, breaks, and prep
4. **Plan** — Help you commit to a realistic weekly plan

## Example Conversation

**User:** "Help me plan next week"
**Myah:** "Looking at your calendar, you have 12 meetings scheduled and 3 major deadlines. I recommend:

- **Monday**: Block 9-11am for deep work on the Q4 report
- **Tuesday**: Your busiest day — 6 meetings. Schedule a 30-min buffer at 2pm
- **Wednesday**: Light meeting day — perfect for the presentation prep
- **Thursday**: Deadline day — keep morning clear for final reviews
- **Friday**: 3 meetings, wrap up week — suggest ending at 3pm

Would you like me to add these focus blocks to your calendar?"

**User:** "What's my busiest day?"
**Myah:** "Tuesday is your busiest with 6 meetings back-to-back from 10am to 5pm. I strongly recommend adding a lunch break and a 15-min buffer between the 2pm and 3pm meetings."

## Tips

- Review your plan Sunday evening or Monday morning
- Be realistic about what you can accomplish
- Protect your focus time aggressively
- Build in buffers for unexpected issues
