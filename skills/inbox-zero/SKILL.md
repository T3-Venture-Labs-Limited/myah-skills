# Inbox Zero

Achieve and maintain inbox zero with intelligent email triage, smart reply drafting, and effective email management strategies.

## Features

- **Smart triage** — Categorize emails by urgency and action required
- **Draft replies** — Generate context-aware response drafts
- **Follow-up tracking** — Identify emails needing follow-up and schedule reminders
- **Newsletter digest** — Summarize newsletters and bulk emails
- **Unsubscribe suggestions** — Identify mailing lists you rarely open

## Workflow

1. **Scan** — Review new emails and categorize them
2. **Triage** — Sort into: Reply Now, Reply Later, Delegate, Archive, Delete
3. **Draft** — Create reply drafts for urgent items
4. **Schedule** — Set follow-up reminders for pending items
5. **Clear** — Archive or delete processed emails

## Example Conversation

**User:** "Help me process my inbox"
**Myah:** "You have 23 unread emails. Here's my triage:

**Reply Now (3):**
- Client meeting request for tomorrow — needs confirmation
- Team member asking about the project deadline
- Invoice requiring approval

**Reply Later (5):**
- Industry newsletter — I'll summarize key points
- Project update — review after lunch
- Meeting notes — no immediate action needed

**Archive (12):**
- Notifications, updates, completed threads

**Delete (3):**
- Spam, expired offers

Shall I draft replies for the urgent items?"

**User:** "Draft a reply to the client meeting request"
**Myah:** "Here's a draft:

---

Hi [Client Name],

Thank you for reaching out. I'm available tomorrow at 2pm or 4pm EST. Would either of those work for you?

Looking forward to catching up.

Best regards

---

Would you like me to adjust the tone or suggest different times?"

## Strategies

- **The 2-minute rule** — If it takes less than 2 minutes, do it now
- **Batch processing** — Handle emails in dedicated blocks, not continuously
- **Template library** — Save common replies for faster responses
- **Scheduled sends** — Draft now, send at optimal times
