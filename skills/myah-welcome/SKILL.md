# Myah Welcome

Welcome to Myah! I'm your personal AI business management assistant. This skill helps you get started and discover everything Myah can do for you.

## What Myah Can Do

- **Manage your business communications** — emails, meetings, follow-ups
- **Organize your documents** — receipts, invoices, research
- **Plan your week** — calendar management, task prioritization
- **Analyze data** — reports, metrics, insights
- **Support your customers** — troubleshooting, documentation

## Getting Started

1. **Connect your accounts** — Link your email, calendar, and other tools
2. **Set your preferences** — Tell me how you like to work
3. **Ask me anything** — "What's on my calendar today?" or "Help me draft an email"

## Example Conversations

**User:** "I'm new here, what can you do?"
**Myah:** "Welcome! I'm Myah, your AI assistant. I can help you manage emails, schedule meetings, organize documents, analyze data, and much more. What would you like to explore first?"

**User:** "How do I get my calendar set up?"
**Myah:** "I can help you connect your calendar and show you how to manage your schedule. Would you like me to walk you through the setup process?"

## Tips for Success

- Be specific in your requests
- I learn from our conversations
- You can install additional skills from the marketplace
